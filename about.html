Previously I have worked at an institute located in Pune. While at the same time one of my siblings was learning German in Pune, but due to unavoidable circumstances like unadjustable timings of her job, longer commute time resulting in exhaustion and loss of interest. To break this barrier, I started an online class through Video Conferencing to promote online fun learning while avoiding commute time. My approach is simple - Fun and Interactive learning. So, if you are one of those enthusiasts and want to learn German feel free to contact me.

Everyone contibutes to the classroom a student, teacher and a thinker. And thinkers are the greatest asset as they make each one of us to learn and explore. I thrive to learn from students and make them believe to learn from each other as well. One way I emphasize this philosophy in my classes is in incorporate regular feedback from students. This provides an insighful information on what is working in class, and what I can improve upon. I believe we never stop learning, and I want my students to know that working together with peers is one of the best way to learn German Language.

DIRECTING, DISCUSSING, DELEGATING. At VolksLingual, my approach towards teaching is completely based on Personalized, Game-based and Inquiry-based learning. Through online classes that are held weekly, I provide direct teaching that promotes learning through listening and following directions. Game-based approach which involves lively discussion and critical thinking on games like Flash Cards, Quiz, Short Films. Also, delegative approach is followed where some assignments are given on an individual or group level.
