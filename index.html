    Why Should You Learn German

    EDUCATION - German universities offer excellent teaching and research, ranking among best in the world. Public universities are free for everyone even non-Germans and this is he main reason that there are over 357,000 foreign students seeking a university degree in Germany whereas the number is steadily increasing. Also, in comparison with other countries, Germany is a safe country.

    Tourism - grab the opportunity to travel in Europe where German is widely spoken , specially in Eastern Europe.

    Culture - get to know the culture and how people live there.

    Career - knowledge of German language increases career opportunities with German and foreign companies.

    Business - knowing the language of your business partners is going to prove beneficial to strengthen the relation and effective communication because you love the language
