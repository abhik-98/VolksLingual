    Why Should You Learn German ?

    EDUCATION - German universities offer excellent teaching and research, ranking among best in the world. Public universities are free for everyone even non-Germans and this is the main reason for over 357,000 foreign students seeking a university degree in Germany, whereas the number is steadily increasing. Also in comparison with other countries, Germany is a safe country.

    TOURISM - Grab the opportunity to travel in Europe where German is widely spoken, specially in Eastern Europe and in the country side. Get to know the culture and how people live there.

    CAREER - Knowledge of German language increases career opportunities with German and foreign companies. Berlin is considered as the second 'silicon valley' in the world, which provides a big opportunity for the growth.

    BUSINESS - Knowing the language of your business partner is going to prove beneficial to strengthen the relation and effective communication because you know the language.
